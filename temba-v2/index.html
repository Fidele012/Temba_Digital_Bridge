Who we serve

Built for communities and providers

Community Members

Residents currently report issues through local leaders with no way to track progress. Temba lets anyone report directly and stay informed — on any phone.

  • Submit water and sanitation reports directly
  • Upload photos or video evidence
  • Track every report from submission to resolution
  • Choose the provider responsible for your area
  • Use USSD — no smartphone or internet needed

Water Service Providers

Providers miss critical issues due to fragmented reporting. Temba gives a single dashboard to receive, respond to, and communicate with communities at scale.

  • Receive and triage reports by urgency level
  • Broadcast alerts by village, cell, or province
  • Call or SMS community members for follow-up
  • Publish water quality and safety notices
  • Manage all communications in one dashboard

About Temba

Built for Rwanda's communities

Temba Digital Bridge is a civic platform built to close the communication gap between Rwandan communities and the water service providers that serve them.

Accessible via web app or USSD on any phone — no smartphone or internet required. Available in English and Kinyarwanda.
